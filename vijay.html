Vijay

Full Name: Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar

Born: 22 June 1974

Occupation: Actor, Singer

Introduction

Vijay is a popular Indian actor who works mainly in Tamil cinema. He is one of the highest-paid actors in India and has a large fan following.

Early Life

Vijay was born in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. His father is a film director, and his mother is a playback singer.

Career

He started his acting career as a child artist and later became a lead actor. He has acted in many successful films and is known for his mass roles.

Popular Movies

  • Ghilli
  • Thuppakki
  • Mersal
  • Master
  • Leo

Awards

Vijay has received several awards for his performances and is widely appreciated for his acting and dancing skills.

Personal Life

He is married and has two children. Vijay is also involved in social activities and charity work.
